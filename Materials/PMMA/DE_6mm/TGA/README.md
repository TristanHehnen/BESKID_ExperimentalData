# Thermogravimetric Analysis (TGA) and Differential Scanning Calorimetry (DSC) Experiments

Samples have been powdered using mortar and pestle.
Experiments were conducted using a NETZSCH STA 449F3 device. Samples were placed in open Al2O3 crucibles with a nominal volume of 85 µl.

Reliable calibration for DSC at heating rates of more than 20 K/min.

For heating rates more than 20 K/min the oven kept overheating during regulation of the temperature close to the target of 700 °C, thus the emergency temperature needed to be increased.
